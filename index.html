Introduction

Welcome to Fazbear Entertainment: The Roleplay (FETRP)! This is a roleplay game based on Five Nights at Freddy's, created by Scott Cawthon. Please be aware that this game is not yet in its final version, so you may encounter bugs and glitches.


Credits

As a fan game of FNaF, all credits go to Scott Cawthon, the creator of the Five Nights at Freddy's franchise, and Steel Wool Studios. Additional credits can be seen in-game.
